What do we mean by Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions.

Do you Know that Globally average Carbon Emission by an individual is 4 tones a year. That's Huge!

To Avoid rise in 2°C in global temperature, the average carbon footprint need to drop by 2 tones by 2050. This can't happen overnight. Remember by making small changes, we can start a Big Difference!
